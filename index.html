About UnPhiltered Trivia

Phil Grant started hosting trivia back in 2021, right around the time he and his wife decided to get married. What began as a side hobby turned into something bigger when Phil launched UnPhiltered Trivia in 2024, just one month before his son Ezra was born.

Today, every dollar of profit from UnPhiltered Trivia goes toward Ezra's education. Every game features original questions, a mix of difficulty levels, and the kind of energy that keeps teams coming back week after week. Whether your crew knows everything about 90s sitcoms or the periodic table, there's a round for you.

UnPhiltered Trivia is also available for private events, corporate team-building, and special occasions. If you can gather a crowd, Phil will bring the game.
